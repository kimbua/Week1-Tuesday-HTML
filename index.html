21. Create a form that asks for:

  • Email, password and confirm password. Each of the field has a arcording placeholder.
  • Title (Radio box), 2 options: Developer or Employer
  • Interested in (Checkbox), 3 options: ReactJS, NodeJS, Web Design
  • City (Select box), 3 options: Hanoi, HCMC, Da Nang
  • Short Bio (Text area)
  • A confirm button which is disabled.

Solution:

          
            <form>
              <label for="email"> Your email:</label><br>
              <input type="text" id="email" name="email" placeholder="Email"><br>
              <label for="password">Password:</label><br>
              <input type="password" id="password" name="password" placeholder="Password"><br>
              <label for="Confirm Password">Confirm Password:</label><br>
              <input type="password" id="Confirm Password" name="Confirm Password" placeholder="Confirm Password"><br>
              <p>Job title:</p>
              <input type="radio" id="developer" name="title" value="developer">
              <label for="developer">Developer</label>
              <input type="radio" id="employer" name="title" value="employer">
              <label for="employer">Employer</label>
              <p>You are interested in:</p>
              <input type="checkbox" id="ReactJS" name="interest" value="ReactJS">
              <label for="ReactJS">ReactJS</label>
              <input type="checkbox" id="NodeJS" name="interest" value="NodeJS">
              <label for="NodeJS">NodeJS</label>
              <input type="checkbox" id="Web Design" name="interest" value="Web Design">
              <label for="Web Design">Web Design</label>
              <p>You are from:</p>
              <select id="city">
                <option value="HN">Ha Noi</option>
                <option value="HCM">Ho Chi Minh City</option>
                <option value="DN">Da Nang</option>
              </select><br><br>
              <label for="bio">Short bio:</label><br>
              <textarea id="bio" name="bio" rows="4" cols="50">
              </textarea><br>
              <input type="submit" value="Submit" disabled>
            </form>
